## About Franklin Mtei

Franklin Mtei is the co-founder of AI4TZA. He is dedicated to advancing the use of Artificial Intelligence for social impact in Tanzania.

## Role at AI4TZA

- Oversees strategic direction
- Drives AI adoption initiatives
- Focuses on education, health, and agriculture innovations

## Vision

Empower Tanzanian communities by leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and foster national development.

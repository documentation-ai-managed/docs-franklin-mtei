---
title: Can't Log In to AI4TZA Platform
description: Quick fix for login problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads or shows error
- Password not accepted

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct. Reset password if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and cache, then try logging in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, email `support@ai4tza.or.tz`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
